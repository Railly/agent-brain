# Q1 2026 Reflection

## What went well
- Shipped the dashboard MVP ahead of schedule
- Built a consistent daily logging habit
- Started using `/pulse` weekly, the blind spot detection is uncomfortable but useful

## What didn't go well
- Content creation: 8 ideas, 2 published. The gap between ideation and execution is real.
- Sleep average dropped to 6.2h in February. Correlated with late-night coding sessions.
- Said yes to too many side projects. Three abandoned repos this quarter.

## Patterns I noticed
- My best work happens between 9am-12pm. Meetings after 2pm kill the rest of the day.
- I write better content when I draft immediately after the experience, not days later.
- The projects I abandoned all shared one trait: no clear user beyond myself.

## Q2 priorities
1. Publish weekly, even if it's short
2. Sleep by midnight, no exceptions
3. Say no to projects without a user
